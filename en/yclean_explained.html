How does YClean work?

In simple steps you can register, make a reservation and have your car cleaned.

Make an account

  • Via the link on our website you go to the registration form where you enter your personal and car details, payment information and your preferences
  • You will receive login details, so you can always log in and change your personal information

Make online reservation

  • Via our website (and app) you can log in and easily make a reservation in our agenda at a time that suits you
  • You will receive a confirmation with date, time and location

The day of service

  • At the day of service, you park your car in the parking of your company (specific location?)
  • You hand over your car keys
  • You can pick up your car keys
  • You will drive home in a clean car

After care and communication

  • If you have remarks, points for improvement, you can contact …
  • 2 days before your next appointment you will receive a reminder
