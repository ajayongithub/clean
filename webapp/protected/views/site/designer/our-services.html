Our service

With our services, we believe that we can offer you convenience and save you time while you are at work! And together we contribute to a better environment

We clean Your car

  • At your convenience: we wash cars at your office at time that suits you
  • Saving you precious time and money: you do not have to wait at service stations buy tickets and stand in line for car washing,wasting precious time you can spend with your family and friends
  • Reducing your ecological footprint: we do so by using high quality bio-degradable cleaning products and we do not use water
  • To ensure a constant enjoyment of car continuous / Sustainable / Steady / Representation: every month a great clean look.

With our offerings, we believe that we can offer you convenience and save you time while you are in the office. During the day we can clean your car on the outside and the inside, whatever you prefer.

We offer two standalone cleaning modules and a combination of the two .

Exterior cleaning

We take pride on the car we drive. A good looking clean car definitely makes us feel better. Also what you use to clean it matters. Our exterior cleaning products are non-toxic, biodegradable and phosphate free.

With our exterior cleaning, your car’s the paint area, the glass area and the wheels, we've got you covered. Our exterior cleaning offers you an effective and earth-friendly solution.

Interior cleaning

Okay let's face it. You spend a lot of time inside your car. A clean car is so much better to drive. Also what you use to clean it matters. Our interior cleaning products are non-toxic, biodegradable and phosphate free.

Our interior cleaning included cleaning your dashboard, your upholstery, glass area and the carpet area. Without water of course. Our cleaning safely removes dirt, grime, bugs, fingerprints, and more.
